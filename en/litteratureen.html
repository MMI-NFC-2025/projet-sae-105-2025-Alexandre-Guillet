Further reading — Recommended books

Literature plays a crucial role in rehabilitating forgotten figures. Books like "Women in Science" by Rachel Ignotofsky revive pioneers and document their essential contributions. These works challenge biases, deconstruct the myth of the solitary male genius, and reveal mechanisms that erased women's achievements from scientific history.
